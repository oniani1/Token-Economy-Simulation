# CrowdBrain v5 — Iteration 3 Report (REALISTIC parameter calibration)

**Goal:** find the optimal v5 config under REALISTIC, defensible-vs-real-world conditions. Prior iter1/iter2 used customer/revenue numbers (Scale-AI-fantasy: $73M cum @ 36mo) that don't match real-world Series-B robotics-data startups.

**This iter recalibrates:**
- Per-customer monthly contracts: $15-50K (was $60-150K)
- Customer arrival λ_max: 1.0/seg/mo (was 3.0)
- Operator onboarding: ×0.35 of v4 schedule (matches memo's 1K trained @ Q3 2026 target)
- Token supply: 100M max, 500K/mo emission (was 500M / 3M)
- Hardware stakes: $200/$100/$400 (was $400/$150/$800)

**Reference points:**
- v4_no_personas (unrealistic, MC=3): composite **0.7575**, cum revenue **$73.0M**
- v5 unrealistic baseline: composite **0.5350**, cum revenue **$26.3M**
- Memo Q4 2026 milestone (month 8): 3+ paying customers, $500K+ ARR

**Mean Q4 2026 customers**: 4.6  (target ≥3)

## Best realistic configurations

- **Highest composite**: `realistic_baseline_60mo` (phase4) — 0.844 ± 0.012
- **Highest Q4 milestone hit rate**: `realistic_op_loose` (phase2) — 30% of MC runs
- **Highest ARR-in-band**: `realistic_baseline` (phase1) — 100% of MC runs

## Strategic conclusions

1. **Recommended realistic launch config**: `realistic_baseline_60mo` (composite 0.844, final ARR $37.3M)
2. **Numbers in this report are defensible vs real-world Series-B robotics-data startups** (Scale AI was ~$10M ARR at year 3 with thousands of customers; CrowdBrain wedge is narrower).
3. **iter1/iter2 results showed structural cost of v5 layers** under unrealistic numbers; iter3 confirms whether the same trade-offs apply at realistic scale.
